Rules and Stuff

Welcome to the Javascript rock, paper, scissors game. To play you must choose either rock, paper, or scissors. The rules are that rock will crush scissors and be covered by paper, thus losing to paper and winning to scissors. Choosing paper will mean that against rock your will win but against scissors you will lose. Scissors, therefore, lose to rock and win against paper.

Champion please choose your weapon!
